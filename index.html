Took 1st and 2nd place in 5.56 at the 2025 Silencer Summit in its debut year. DMLS Inconel, XCR backpressure tech, HUB/1.375×24 compatible, full-auto rated, 6ARC capable. The most interesting can at the event — priority demo.

All Vendors at OSF 2026

For reference — backup options if primary demos aren’t available

Dead Air Primary Pick
Sandman-S (full-size workhorse, KeyMo QD), Nomad-Ti (lighter K-adjacent option). Top-tier QD ecosystem.
EchoCore Primary Pick
Sector 5.56 Compact (K-size, 2025 Summit winner), Sector 5.56 Full Size (#1 overall at Summit). Brand new, DMLS Inconel, HUB compatible — biggest surprise at the event.
HuxWrx Primary Pick
Flow 556K (flow-through design, reduced backpressure). Notably different performance profile than conventional baffled cans — a direct comparison is worth a demo token.
SilencerCo
Omega 36M (multi-cal, excellent all-rounder). Good mainstream benchmark — worth handling at the vendor table even without a demo token.
Rugged Suppressors
Razor 556 (lightweight titanium), Surge 762 (multi-cal, dual-taper lock). Solid value-tier, full-auto rated. Good backup if a primary slot opens.
Otter Creek Labs
Polonium K 556. You own one — skip the demo token, just chat with the rep.
Q
Trash Panda (22LR/multi), Honey Badger (integrally suppressed). Q is less 5.56-focused; interesting brand but lower priority for your goals.
SureFire
SOCOM556-RC2 (full-size, military heritage), SOCOM556-Mini (compact). Built tough, proven platform. Worth handling if you have a free moment.
Canik
Suppressor lineup is pistol-focused. Lowest priority for a 5.56 demo token; skip unless you’re curious about their pistol cans.
